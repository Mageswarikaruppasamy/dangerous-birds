CASSOWARY BIRD


    Cassowaries are flightless birds.Flightless birds are birds that lost their ability to fly through evolution. Cassowaries are native to the tropical forests of New Guinea , the Aru Islands ,northeastern Australia. Southern cassowary is most common and known as most dangerous bird on the earth.It is the third-tallest and second-heaviest living bird, smaller only than the ostrich and emu , weigh up to 157 lbs.This deadliest bird on Earth has made hundreds of attacks on humans. Most of these attacks were the result of humans trying to feed or otherwise interact with these large birds. Its most dangerous characteristics are:

  1. the three claws that grow from each foot extends to five inches
  2. their kicks are enough to break bones and kill
Despite being such dangerous birds , anthropological evidence indicates that they were domesticated by humans 18,000 years ago.
